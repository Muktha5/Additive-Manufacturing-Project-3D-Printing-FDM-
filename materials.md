# 🧱 Materials Used in 3D Printing

## 🔹 Common Materials
- PLA – Easy to print, low cost
- ABS – Strong and durable
- Nylon – High strength and flexibility
- PC (Polycarbonate) – High temperature resistance

---

## ⚙️ Material Properties
- Tensile strength
- Thermal resistance
- Flexibility
- Impact resistance

---

## 📊 Material Selection Criteria
- Application requirements
- Strength vs weight
- Cost
- Printability
