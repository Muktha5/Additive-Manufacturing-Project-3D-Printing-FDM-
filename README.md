# 🧪 Additive Manufacturing Project (3D Printing – FDM)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents hands-on experience and technical understanding of Additive Manufacturing (3D Printing), focusing on Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) using an industrial-grade Stratasys F170 printer.

The work includes understanding the complete workflow from CAD design to final printed product, along with machine operation, calibration, material selection, and post-processing techniques.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand additive manufacturing principles and workflow
- Operate and calibrate industrial 3D printers
- Study material behavior and printing parameters
- Analyze print quality and process efficiency

---

## 🏭 Technology Used
- FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling)
- Layer-by-layer material deposition
- Thermoplastic filament-based printing

---

## 🖨 Machine Used
Stratasys F170 (Industrial FDM Printer)

---

## 🔄 Workflow
1. CAD Design
2. STL Conversion
3. Pre-processing (slicing & setup)
4. Printing (layer-by-layer deposition)
5. Post-processing (support removal & finishing)

---

## 📊 Key Learnings
- Importance of calibration in print accuracy
- Effect of temperature and material on quality
- Role of support structures in complex designs
- Trade-off between speed, strength, and surface finish

---

## 🚀 Applications
- Automotive prototyping
- Aerospace components
- Medical models
- Industrial tooling
