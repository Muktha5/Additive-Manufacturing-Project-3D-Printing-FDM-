# 🧠 Key Insights from Additive Manufacturing

## 🔹 Process Understanding
- Additive manufacturing allows complex geometries not possible in traditional methods

## 🔹 Efficiency
- Reduces material waste compared to subtractive manufacturing
- Faster prototyping and iteration

## 🔹 Limitations
- Surface finish may require post-processing
- Strength varies based on print direction (anisotropy)

## 🔹 Practical Learning
- Calibration is critical for print accuracy
- Material selection directly impacts performance

## 🔹 Industry Relevance
- Widely used in automotive, aerospace, and medical industries
- Increasing role in rapid prototyping and customized production
