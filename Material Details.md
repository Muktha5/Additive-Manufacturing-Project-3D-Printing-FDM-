# 🖨 Stratasys F170 – Machine Details

## 📌 Overview
The Stratasys F170 is an industrial-grade FDM 3D printer used for producing functional prototypes and end-use parts.

---

## ⚙️ Key Components
- Extruder (material deposition)
- Build platform (platen)
- Material bay (filament storage)
- Oven chamber (temperature control)
- Tip wipe system (cleaning nozzle)

---

## 🔧 Calibration
Calibration ensures:
- Accurate material deposition
- Proper alignment between model and support material
- High-quality print output

Types:
- Automatic calibration
- Manual calibration

---

## 🛠 Maintenance
- Regular cleaning of build chamber
- Inspection of nozzle and tip
- Removal of material debris
